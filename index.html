VIZIANAGARAM District is predominantly an agricultural district as 68.4% of the workers are engaged in Agriculture and about 82% of the population of the District is living in Rural area s and depend on agriculture for their livelihood. Rainfed farming is the characteristic of Agriculture in the District as about 80% of its area is cultivated purely under Rainfed conditions. Even the rest of the area which is termed as irrigated area is mostly dependent on the rainfall received in the District.The technologies that has to be used in the plantation and to plant the suitable crop in the certain locality are:

  1. Forest gardening
  2. Wild simulated
  3. Forest tending
  4. Wildcrafting
  5. Harvest automation
  6. Seeding and weeding
  7. Autonomous tractors
